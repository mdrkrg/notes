## ALU
The ALU computes a function on the two inputs, and outputs the result

![[ALU.svg]]
- $f$ : one out of a family of **pre-defined** arithmetic and logical functions
	- Arithemetic: integer addition, multiplication, division
	- Locigal: And, Or, Xor, ...
